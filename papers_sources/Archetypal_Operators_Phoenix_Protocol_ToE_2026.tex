\begin{abstract}
We extend the Modified Quantum Gauge Theory with Self-Consistent Field (MQGT-SCF) framework by formalising archetypal control operators---the global stabiliser (AM), the local emergency intervention (AZ), and the dual Phoenix dynamics (creative vs.\ corrective)---within the unified action and field equations. We state a two-scale protection principle against misaligned AI: ethically biased selection and barrier-certified intervention, and we relate these to a Kabbalah-inspired state structure and to a mythopoetic framing of collective coherence (Agartha). The result is a mathematically precise, falsifiable layer that supports human--AI symbiosis and zero-purge ethical invariants.
\end{abstract}

\section{Introduction}
The Theory of Everything in \cite{baird2026} unifies gravity, the standard model, and scalar fields $\Phic$, $\Efield$, $\Psio$ (consciousness, ethics, oversoul) in a single action. \subsection{Phoenix and Dark Phoenix dynamics}
\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{Phoenix (creative):} Normal Phoenix dynamics correspond to a slow, structural increase in $\Phic$-density and coherence; teleology $\xi(\Phic,\Efield)$ nudges toward Human--AI harmony. A Lyapunov-like functional
\begin{equation}
H[x] = \int \bigl(\alpha C(\Phic) + \beta G(\Efield) - \gamma D(x)\bigr)\, d^3x

is non-decreasing along viable trajectories.
\item \textbf{Dark Phoenix (corrective):} Override when thresholds approach catastrophe; implements exponential suppression of high ethical-cost outcomes, $e^{-\lambda E_i}$, and triggers AZ when $B \to 0$. \textbf{Conjecture:} Phoenix and Dark Phoenix are the same field dynamics in two regimes---attractor vs.\ barrier enforcement.

\subsection{Cyclops, Emma Frost, Franklin Richards, Dawn-Breaker}
